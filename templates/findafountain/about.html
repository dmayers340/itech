FAQ

  • What is Find a Fountain?


    Find a Fountain is a group project by the Thirsty Pythons for the Internet Technology course at the University of Glasgow

  • Why choose Find a Fountain?


    As eco-friendly students, we bring our own reusable water bottles to campus, however, it can be difficult to find places to fill the water bottles up. We thought an app like Find a Fountain would help students find bubblers in order to drink more water
